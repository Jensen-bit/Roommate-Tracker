### 4. Application Name
Roommate-Tracker

### 5. Application Description
UPDATE IF NEEDED:
Roommate-Tracker is a centralized hub designed to manage the chaos of shared living. It provides a shared dashboard where roommates can track rotating chores, manage shared grocery lists, and stay updated on household announcements. 

The app aims to eliminate the friction of "who forgot to do the dishes" by providing a transparent, digital record of responsibilities. It ensures that every member of the household is on the same page regarding daily tasks and long-term household needs.

### 6. Audience
UPDATE IF NEEDED:
Our target audience is college students and young professionals living in communal environments. The app is built for users who need a low-friction way to stay organized amidst busy academic or professional schedules.

### 7. Vision Statement
UPDATE IF NEEDED:
For students in shared housing who struggle with accountability, the Roommate-Tracker is a household management tool that automates chore tracking and communication. Unlike messy group chats or forgotten whiteboards, our product provides a dedicated, validated system for maintaining a clean and organized home.
