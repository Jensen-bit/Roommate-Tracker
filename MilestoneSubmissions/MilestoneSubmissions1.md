### 4. Application Name
FairShare

### 5. Application Description
The app tracks shared living expenses between users‚ letting them record‚ split‚ and track household-related expenses like housing‚ bills‚ groceries‚ and other commodities․ When making a payment‚ users can opt to split the payment with various people specified by the app․ The app has a monthly fee-splitting feature‚ which calculates the monthly amounts owed between roommates‚ and shows their balances to each other in real-time‚ eliminating the need for awkward conversations when settling bills․

The application has built-in notifications which alert users of financial obligations such as when a new expense is added‚ when an amount is owed from the user‚ when a payment is to be paid‚ or when a transaction is completed․ It can also eliminate missed payments and keep all roommates informed and accountable‚ providing a more organized‚ transparent budget‚ and more relaxed living arrangement․

### 6. Audience
The application's target audience is college students living with a roommate․ Users usually are between the ages of 18 and 30․ They live together‚ splitting the cost of housing‚ utilities‚ and groceries․ The main problem we want to solve is the hassle involved in splitting expenses‚ currently done manually through spreadsheets‚ notes‚ etc․‚ leading to users occasionally missing expenses and not knowing what they owe and to who. Our app also tracks all the expenses‚ works out who owes who and when‚ letting all those involved see the information in real time․ Notifications will alert users that they owe someone money‚ that a payment is coming due or that a payment has been received․ The software will have a clear‚ clean and mobile-friendly interface‚ and will include easy-to-read payment summaries‚ simple payment entry and notification options to make managing and repaying shared debts and expenses less stressful‚ no matter where the app is being used․

### 7. Vision Statement
For college students and young adults living with roommates who need a simple way to track and manage shared expenses, FairShare is a roommate expense tracking application that automatically calculates balances, tracks payments, and sends real-time notifications to keep everyone accountable. Unlike spreadsheets or scattered payment apps, our product provides a centralized, easy-to-use platform with built-in reminders that eliminates confusion and ensures everyone pays their fair share on time.
